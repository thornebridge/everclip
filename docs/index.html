What it does

Everything you copy,
organized forever.

🔍

Smart detection

Auto-recognizes text, URLs, code, images, colors, Markdown, and files. Each gets its own preview.

📌

Pinboards & tags

Color-coded collections. Multi-board. Items live in as many places as you need them.

⚡

Smart rules

Auto-route clips to pinboards by app, type, regex, or domain. Zero manual sorting.

📚

Paste stack

Collect multiple copies, paste them all in sequence. Batch workflows, perfected.

🔄

Transformations

Paste as plain text, UPPERCASE, lowercase, URL encoded, trimmed. Right-click any clip.

🔎

Instant search

Find anything. Filter by type, app, or full-text search across your entire history.

⌨️

Text expansion

Snippets with {{date}}, {{time}}, {{clipboard}} templates. Type less.

🔒

Privacy first

Everything on your Mac. Exclude sensitive apps. Pause anytime. Your clipboard, your rules.

🔐

Credential vault

Right-click any clip and save it as a username or password. Organized by platform, passwords auto-clear from clipboard after 30 seconds.
